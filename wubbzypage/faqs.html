Q: How come I can’t get the bonus features to open on my computer?

A: Don’t worry, all your bonus features are there!  Please follow these steps to get to the activities, coloring pages and game from your DVD-ROM.

  • Insert the DVD into the DVD drive on your computer.
  • Go to Start and then My Computer.
  • In the list you should see:  Wow_Wow_Wubbzy (D:)
  • Right click this and choose either OPEN or EXPLORE.

This will bring you to a list where you can open all of the files, including coloring pages, activities and the invitations. 

  • To play the Game clicking on the Firefox document "Click to Play.html".  From there you will be directed to a web page with the Wubbzy Game.

Q: Will you be coming out with a Wubbzy DVD that has never-before-seen episodes?

A: The Wubbzy Team definitely agrees that it would be great to release a DVD with a never-before-seen episode and our hope is that we can offer fans this potential opportunity on future DVDs.  Unfortunately, timing on the production of new episodes did not allow for this on our first and second DVDs.  The second one comes out on February 3rd, 2009 and while the episodes have been shown on TV, we did try to make it a Friendship themed DVD where all of the episodes focus on Friendship and how to be a good friend.  We definitely love the idea of new episodes and will keep it in mind!

Questions about the Wubbzy website -  wubbzy.com

Q: I was hoping there would be more on wubbzy.com, like videos and games, where are they?

A: Wubbzy.com is in the process of updating its site with tons of awesome activities, games, information and more for all of our fabulous Wubbzy fans.  Please be sure to keep checking back for the new site, full of wubbtastic fun!  It should be up soon – late October if all goes well!

Q: When I click “Play Wubbzy Games Now” on the homepage it asks if I have a Wubb Code.  What is this?

A: A Wubb Code is a secret code inside all Fisher Price products.  If you have a code you can play all of the games on the site.  If you don’t have a code, don’t worry!  By clicking “NO” you can still play Wubbzy’s Sketchity Sketch Pad game.

Q: I would love to receive updates on new Wubbzy DVDs as well as exclusive offers.  Is this possible?

A: Definitely!  With the launch of the new wubbzy.com website there will be a brand-new section called Join the WubbClub, where parents can register to receive the latest Wubbzy exclusive news updates via email.  Included in the monthly newsletters will be information on the next DVD, activities, Wubbzy recipes, crafts and even a little note from Wubbzy himself!  For signing up for the WubbClub parents can print a customized Wubbzy membership card and certificate exclusively for their little ones!  If you have any ideas of what information is most important to you, or ideas for the newsletter please let us know by clicking on CONTACT US on the homepage.  Please address all emails regarding the newsletter to Jennifer.
