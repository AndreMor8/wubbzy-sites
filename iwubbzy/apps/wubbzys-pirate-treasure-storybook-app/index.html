Wow! Wow! Wubbzy’s Pirate Treasure Storybook App for Kids

Join Wubbzy and his friends in this fun-filled storybook app. Kids and parents will enjoy! Available on iPad, iPhone, Android & Kindle Fire.

Wubbzy’s Pirate Adventure is an animated storybook with over 100 fun surprises hidden throughout the pages. Your child can have Wubbzy read to them or you can read it yourself! Packed within this playful app are tons of music videos (including the famous Wubbzy Wiggle) and fun mini-games! If you’re just in the mood to read you can also use the “Just A Book” option too.

Join Wubbzy and his friends in this fun-filled storybook adventure from the Emmy® Award winning Nick Jr. ® television series Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! ® Wubbzy and his pals Widget, Walden, and Daizy love to play and learn together through all their wonderful adventures in Wuzzleburg. Join Wubbzy and his friends on an amazing adventure with pirates, snappity-snap turtles, boat rides, dancing, and more!

Help Wubbzy, Widget, Daizy, and Walden find a special (and surprising) pirate treasure that teaches them the value of friendship.

This children's app includes a Wubbzy storybook, 4 games and 3 lively original music videos, and features 3 reading modes.

READ & PLAY
In this mode, as you read the story to your child, they can tap and interact on each page finding fun and hidden surprises!

FUN GAMES – INSIDE THE STORY!
Four mini-games within the storybook to further engage your child’s imagination.  Pirate Dress-Up, Froggity-Frogs, Rockity-Rocks and Snappity-Snap Turtles.

SING ALONG MUSIC VIDEOS
There are three Wubbtastic Music Videos for your child (and you) to sing and dance along too! The three music videos included with this app are Pirate Treasure, You’re A Star, and The Wubbzy Wiggle. Wow Wow Wow!

BOOKPLATE PERSONALIZATION
Add your child’s picture and name to the main menu while playing with Wubbzy’s Pirate Adventure! Make your child an honorary citizen of Wuzzleburg!

READ TO ME
Our narrator reads the book and your child can tap and interact with the story.

JUST A BOOK
This mode works like a good, old-fashioned picture book.  It turns off interaction and sound for reading time.  Perfect for bedtime and quiet time with your child.
