Wubbzy’s Train Adventure – Available Now

Toot Toot! In the new storybook app, Wubbzy’s Train Adventure,

Toot Toot! In the new storybook app, Wubbzy’s Train Adventure, Wubbzy and his friends take a ride on the Wuzzleburg Express. Destined for their favorite Kooky Karnival, the characters must overcome fun, silly and unexpected obstacles when the train breaks down. Can the wuzzle friends get the train back on track before the fair closes?

Available for:
iPad and Android
  • Features
  • Reviews

Wow! Wow! Wow! features include 3 reading modes

  • READ & PLAY In this mode, as you read the story to your child, they can tap and interact on each page.
  • READ TO ME A narrator reads the book and your child can tap and interact with the story.
  • JUST A BOOK This mode works like a good, old-fashioned picture book. It turns off interaction and sound. Perfect for bedtime and quiet time with your child.

Other Features

  • PAUSE BUTTON: Allows kids return to game play whenever they want to.
  • FUN GAMES INSIDE THE STORY Two mini-games, Track Build-Up and Kooky Karnival Fun, are built into the story to further engage your child’s imagination.
  • SING ALONG MUSIC VIDEOS: Tour child will want to get up and dance with three original music videos: Are You Ready?, Wubbzy Wiggle and Me and My Friends.
  • COLORING: Kids can tap into their creativity by painting some of their favorite characters straight from the story!
