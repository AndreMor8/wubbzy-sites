Wubbzy's Dinosaur Adventure

NEW EDUCATIONAL FEATURES ADDED

  • Hands-On Teaching Activities
  • Educational Experiences Enhanced with Common Core State Standards

Wow! Wow! Dinosaurs! Join Wubbzy and friends in this interactive story experience that takes young readers on a fun-filled adventure all the way to Dino Island! See what happens when Wubbzy discovers that dinosaurs aren't so scary after all!

BONUS LEARNING ACTIVITIES

Wubbzy's Dinosaur Adventure will have children and their parents laughing and learning with their favorite characters from the Emmy® Award-winning Nick Jr. TV series Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!®. Using hands-on teaching activities, developed by seasoned educators, to emphasize and reinforce essential skill building for Matching, Counting, Reading and Site Word Recognition. Kids can also tap into their creative skills as they decorate cupcakes, paint storybook pages and much, much more!

The story includes professional narration, promoting reading through sound and sight. Parents will appreciate the Grown-Up's Corner, which provides thoughtful questions designed to spark conversation while promoting critical thinking and comprehension.

IINFUSED WITH COMMON CORE STATE STANDARDS

Also included is the NEW Common Core Corner for further explanation of the educational activities infused within the story to help prepare children for the Common Core State Standards (CCSS) they’ll be expected to know in school. This is a new feature being added to all Cupcake Digital deluxe story experiences. For more educator-developed activities inspired by the story and designed to practice CCSS skills, please visit cupcakedigital.com.

Wubbzy’s Dinosaur Adventure was developed by Cupcake Digital in partnership with the producers of the Emmy® Award-winning Nick Jr. TV series Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!©.

Wow! Wow! Wow! features include 3 reading modes

  • READ & PLAY In this mode, as you read the story to your child, they can tap and interact on each page.
  • READ TO ME A narrator reads the book and your child can tap and interact with the story.
  • JUST A BOOK This mode works like a good, old-fashioned picture book. It turns off interaction and sound. Perfect for bedtime and quiet time with your child.

Other Features Include

  • EXCEPTIONAL LEARNING ACTIVITIES INSIDE THE STORY Dino Matching and Cupcake Craze, are built into the story to further engage a child’s imagination, including bonus Learning Games -- Dino Alphabet, Dino Numbers and Dino Words – to reinforce writing, counting, and letter or word recognition.
  • SING ALONG MUSIC VIDEOS Your child will want to get up and dance with original music videos: You Are Unique, Wubbzy Wiggle and Robot Dance!
  • COLORING Kids can tap into their creativity by painting some of their favorite characters and scenes straight from the story!
