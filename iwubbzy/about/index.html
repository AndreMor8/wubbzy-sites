About Us

“Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!,” a worldwide hit with young viewers and their families since its 2006 TV premiere on Nickelodeon®, currently airs daily on Nick Jr. Wubbzy is a fun, lovable, curious little guy who just likes to play, play, play with his friends in the eye-popping town of Wuzzleburg. The top-rated series supports preschool social skills – sharing, listening, compromising, helping and appreciating others, self-awareness and the use of humor as a coping skill.

Although their world is more fanciful and imaginative than our own, Wubbzy and his friends deal with the same social and developmental issues faced by preschoolers. They learn how they fit into a group, to understand their own feelings and those of others, to get along with one another and about the value of friendship and community.

“Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!” is produced by Bolder Media, Inc. in association with Starz Media’s Film Roman Animation Studio. It is a worldwide hit that is broadcast in 100+ countries. Susan Miller Lazar (co-producer of Ella Enchanted) and Fred Seibert (executive producer of The FairlyOdd Parents) are the executive producers of the show created by Emmy Award winner Bob Boyle.

 About Cupcake Digital

Cupcake Digital, Inc. was formed in June 2012 under the guiding principle that a digital
experience designed to make kids smile can also make parents happy. When a child interacts with a tablet, smartphone or e-reader we know they are being given a way to play, laugh, learn and grow. We love being part of that experience.

The principals in Cupcake Digital have collaborated since the spring of 2011 on a variety of
projects, including Wubbzy’s Pirate Treasure, the first app based on the Emmy Award winning Nick Jr. ® Series Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! ®. The app is a top-ranked digital storybook on iTunes and Amazon, and is being followed by additional books and games inspired by Wubbzy and his friends.  Cupcake Digital is already partnering with other major entertainment entities for the exclusive development of storybook and game apps based on their brands and properties.

We have fine-tuned the process of taking existing TV shows, characters, books, movies, etc. and creating comprehensive marketing ecosystems around them. We integrate the writing, designing, programming, and marketing of an app within a cohesive digital platform, including a website and all social media channels. Our goal is to build and maintain a fan base for the app, the source that inspired it, and future projects.

Cupcake is headquartered in NYC, and founded by proven professionals with backgrounds in technology, family entertainment, publishing and brand marketing.

About Bolder Media, Inc.

Bolder Media, Inc. is a joint venture formed by veteran producers Susan Miller and Fred Seibert for the development of books, television series, movies and digital media for young boys and girls. Susan Miller of Bolder Media, Inc. and Mixed Media Group and Fred Seibert of Bolder Media, Inc. and Frederator Studios, are executive producers on “Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!.” Bob Boyle is the creator and executive producer of the series. (www.boldermedia.com)
