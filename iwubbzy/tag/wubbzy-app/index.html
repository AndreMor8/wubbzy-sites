Wow Wow Wubbzy – The Dance Party Dance!

Home | By iWubbzy | April 25, 2013

Get ready to dance and sing! It’s a Wow Wow Wubbzy Dance Party!  Wubbzy and his friends love to sing and dance and that’s why Madam Zabinga came over to the Wubb Clup to throw a  wonderful dance party!

Wubbzy learned a lot about listening to others and playing together in his Dance Party adventure. You will play, learn, and have fun with Wow Wow Wubbzy at the Dance Party!

What is your favorite dance? Wubbzy loves classic dances like ballet, jazz and tap. He likes to dance with partners when he does the salsa, the waltz or the foxy foxtrot! He even likes to freestyle to funky beats. There was that one time when he even tried to break dance! That… did not go so well! Feel free to post pictures, videos and comments about your very favorite dance moves.  Your favorite little yellow friend  loves when you share with him, just as much as he loves sharing with you!

Wubbzy loves dressing up for dance parties. But what should he wear? Should he wear a technicolor wig? Should he wear his gold chain like in his awesome rap video? There is no right or wrong way to dress at a dance party, as long as you are having fun! You can join in at a fun, interactive Wuzzleburg dance party on your tablet and smartphone in the new dance party app, “Wubbzy’s Dance Party”
