Wubbzy’s St. Paddy’s Day Marshmallow Pops!

Recipes | By iWubbzy | March 12, 2013

Wubbzy loves St. Patrick’s day! St. Patrick’s Day is an Irish holiday celebrated all around the globe to honor Saint Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland.

Wubbzy has been looking all over Wuzzleburg for clovers to celebrate this special holiday. In Ireland on St. Patrick’s Day, people traditionally wear a small bunch of shamrocks on their jackets or caps. Children wear orange, white and green badges, and women and girls wear green ribbons in their hair. Wubbzy has a green hat to wear on St. Patricks Day this year!

You know Wubbzy loves marshmallows! Here is the recipe for Wubbzy’s favorite St. Patrick’s day treat:  Wubbzy’s  St. Paddy’s Day Marshmallow Pops!

Wubbzy’s very favorite part about St. Patricks day is Wuzzleburg’s St. Patrick’s day parade! Many cities have a St. Patrick’s Day parade. Dublin, the capital of Ireland, has a huge St. Patrick’s Day festival from March 15-19, that features a parade, family carnivals, treasure hunt, dance, theatre and more. In North American, parades are often held on the Sunday before March 17. Some paint the yellow street lines green for the day! In Chicago, the Chicago River is dyed green with a special dye that only lasts a few hours. There has been a St. Patrick’s Day parade in Boston, Massachusetts since 1737. Montreal is home to Canada’s longest running St. Patrick’s Day parade, which began in 1824.

Check out another one of Wubbzy’s Favorite Parades, Wuzzleburg’s Friendship Day Parade, in Wubbzy’s cool new app “Wubbzy Loves You.”

Wow Wow Wubbzy’s Poppity Popcorn Recipe

Home, Wubbzy Says | By iWubbzy | July 10, 2012

Wubbzy loves making food that is not only delicious, but also fun to eat! These wubbtastic Poppity Popcorn Balls are tasty, fun to eat, and fun to make.Wubbzy loves incorporating his favorite food into everything he makes. Wubbzy is super-duper lucky that his favorite food grows in the Marshmallow forest right next to his house. You guessed it! Wubby’s favorite food is Marshmallows!

 

Wubbzy’s delicious Poppity Popcorn Balls incorporate the poppity-popping fun of popcorn and the delectable deliciousness of marshmallowy marshmallows into one of Wubbzy’s favorite treats.

 

Wubbzy loves getting creative in the kitchen.  After the tough parts that kids and parents can do together, kid’s will love Wubbzy’s favorite part of this recipe:  making poppity-popcorn balls by rolling them in your hands! Wubbzy loves making poppity-popcorn balls of all shapes and sizes! You can even make Popity-popcorn snowmen.  Add a beak and feet to make Poppity Popcorn Birdy Birds.

 

Making Wubbzy Poppity Popcorn Balls is almost as fun as eating them. Wubbzy likes to save a few from each batch  he makes, wrap them in pretty celephane, tie them with a bow and give them as gifts to his friends in Wuzzleburg. Poppity Popcorn balls are even more fun when you share. Wow Wow! Wubbzy Poppity Popcorn Balls are sure to be a hit in any household.
