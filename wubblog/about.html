Biography

Bob Boyle is the creator of Nick Jr. animated show and Random House children's book WOW! WOW! WUBBZY!.

(Both the show and book are scheduled to premiere in spring/fall of 2006 and are being produced by Fred Seibert and Susan Miller of Frederator Studios and Bolder Media.)

Before starting production on this project Bob was a Producer and Art Director on 'The Fairly OddParents' and 'Danny Phantom' for Nickelodeon animation studios.

Bob is also the creator and Executive Producer of an action-comedy series currently in Production at Walt Disney TV Animation.

He has also created 2 original cartoons of his own for Nickelodeon's 'Oh Yeah! Cartoons' series (another Frederator production).

Before plunging into the world of animation, Bob worked as an illustrator in New York. His work has appeared in publications such as the NY Times, Business Week, and The Nation.

Bob lives in beatiful Pasadena California with his longtime girlfriend Teri.
