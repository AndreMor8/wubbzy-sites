WARNING: This Post May Be Too Cute For Some Viewers


After spending some deep reflection on Walden's many fashions last week, I started to think about our other characters. Walden is so suave and debonair in his fashions. Wubbzy, however, takes fashion to the next level. We really see his personality coming out, whether he's setting sail across the seven seas, entertaining his adoring fans, or ...well... just making a mess of himself, Wubbzy is a true guerilla fashionista. This revolutionary approach to fashion makes him not only a fore-runner in the fashion biz, but also too cute to handle! - Carrie

Walden - A Man of Many Looks


One can hardly disagree... Walden is a man of many looks. Throughout our Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! episodes, he appears in SO many different costumes, getups and interesting arrangements. All of the gang does, of course, but I think Walden is a particularly good sport about it. For such a sophisticated guy, he knows how to keep it real. He knows when the cap and gown are appropriate, and when the time is right for his viking outfit. Let's hand it to Walden for keeping us on the lookout for new and different fashion statements! - Carrie
