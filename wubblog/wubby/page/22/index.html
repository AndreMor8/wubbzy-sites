The Sun is Shining on Wubbzy


We're starting to get some really great reviews in from the show. Mekeisha Madden Toby of The Detroit News gave Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! a great review. She writes: "Friends really can make life better. That is the central theme on Nick Jr.'s sweet and light little show, 'Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!' The new cartoon debuts today and follows Wubbzy, a cheerful and good-natured creature who has two great friends -- Widget and Walden. The trio lives in a colorful world called Wuzzleburg.
