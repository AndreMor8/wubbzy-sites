Crochet Wubbzy


I'm not sure if I've blogged about the awesome craft books that have been published for Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! through Leisure Arts. They are a great resource for the DIY-er that wants to make some Wubbster stuff at home.
Anyway, for the first time I stumbled upon a great completed crocheted Wubbzy when I found this image on Flickr! This one comes from Rose over at Tasty Crochet. Rose is a crochet superstar, she has even published her own book called Tasty Crochet. I'm not 100% sure if she used this specific pattern, but it's still cool to see this little fella all whipped up. Has anyone else out there made any Wubbzy goodies with these patterns? If so, send me some pictures! - Carrie

Daizy Plush!


Haha, it's amazing how there is so much going on here in Wuzzleburg that even I can't keep track! I just found out about two new awesome toys that are available. The first is a Daizy Plush. There aren't too many Daizy goodies out there yet, so snatch this one up! This cool plush isn't only super-cute, but when  you squeeze her tummy her pony tails spin.
Also check out the Soapity Suds Wubbzy. According to the site, if you pour soap on Wubbzy's head and squeeze his belly, it creates a soapy hair-do for him that you can use for your scrub-a-dubbin'. How awesome is that? haha
I was excited about these new toys and couldn't wait to share them. So far I've only seen them at the Nick Jr Store, but post a comment below if you've found these toys elsewhere! - Carrie

The New DVD Is Out!

 Hey folks, Guess what! There is a new Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! DVD available. It's out right now and you can buy it in stores or online at sites like Amazon.com and Walmart.com. This DVD, A Little Help From My Friends, has 8 whole episodes for you and your family to watch.

They are:
A Little Help From Your Friends
A Clean Sweep
Mr. Cool
Wubbzy In The Middle
Gidget The Super Robot
Broken Record
Widget Gets The Blooey Blues
Where's My Wiggle Wrench?

Also keep an eye out for another DVD release on March 24th, called Pirate Treasure. This DVD will have 5 episodes, PLUS 2 bonus episodes - one of which has never been seen before! You can actually pre-order the DVD now, so snatch it up while you can!! - Carrie
