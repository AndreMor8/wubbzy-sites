Animation Extreme Makeover

We had a meeting today to talk about some really tough decisions that you have to make when starting up a new production.

1- What color should the walls be?
2- What kind of furniture do you need?
3- Where do I park?

We are actually going to try to spruce up the environment a bit.
I used photoshop (in the photo above) to create my extreme make-over version.

A bigger decision to make is: Mac or PC?
