The Art Of Writing


A couple of weeks ago, some of the writers at the Nickelodeon Animation Studio in Burbank mounted a gallery show composed soley of script pages. They were mostly mock pages created just to poke fun at the writing process.

The page shown above is a page from a My Life As A Teenage Robot script that has some fun with the notes that writers often get on their scripts.

As far as the Wubby writing goes, our first two scripts are approved and I am boarding the first show as we speak.

The image below were just warm up drawings that don't apply to anything. And the only board paper I had was from the Fairly OddParents. We better settle on a name for the show soon so that we can make our own storyboard template.
