Title Card Crazy!



We're closing in on delivering our first show so that means it's title card time. I'm not sure but I think we may be the first pre-school show to use classic cartoon title cards. Even if we're not, title cards are always fun to do. These ones were designed by Steve Daye and painted up real pretty by the lovely and talented Paige Pooler.
