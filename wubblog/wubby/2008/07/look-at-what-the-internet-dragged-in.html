Look at What the Internet Dragged In...

So the unfortunate thing about releasing all of these great goodies is that a lot of times, we don't really know for sure when things will hit the stores exactly. As far as I know, just about everything we've shown you guys here is due to come out in August this year, but honestly, I couldn't tell you whether it was early, mid or late August :( Sorry. But here's a tip:
GOOGLE! In searching for fun pictures and home projects to post, I found this site that seems to be bulk selling party supplies. That tells me that the party supplies are hitting warehouses, so you should be able to find them in regular stores awfully soon. You'll also start to find things like Wacky Wubbzy and Digi Wubbzy on sites like Amazon.com So get googling and see what you can dig up! If you find them somewhere, leave us a comment! - Carrie
