Wow! Wow! Wubbzy loves the Annies!


Wubbzy couldn't be happier right now, because he just found out that Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! has been nominated in the Annie Awards for "Best Animated Television Production". The Annie Awards is a special awards show just for animation, and being recognized is a big deal. So thanks to everyone at the Annie Awards. Just being nominated is exciting, but keep your fingers crossed for your favorite little yellow friend! - Carrie
