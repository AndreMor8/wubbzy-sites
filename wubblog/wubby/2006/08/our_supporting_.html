Our Supporting Cast


Since everyone seemed to enjoy my posts about the many looks of Wubbzy and Walden, I thought it would be fun to look at some of our other characters (don't worry, Widget will get her turn too!). I thought it would be fun to look at some of Wubbzy, Widget and Walden's friends and other citizens of Wuzzleburg. Above are some of the characters we see often. Earl, Buggy, "the Kooky Kid", and Huggy. There are also characters we meet in Wuzzleberg like the chef and policeman. Even little old men and women! It's funny how some of these characters tend to end up in the funniest of places. The poor little old lady (or her pet poodle) seems to end up in the middle all kinds of whacky situations, and "the Kooky Kid" is a goofy character we see in unexpected places too! Maybe later I'll post some of their sillier appearances! - Carrie
