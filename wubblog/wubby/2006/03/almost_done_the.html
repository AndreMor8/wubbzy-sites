Almost done... the Script


Once the whole team is comfortable with the story in outline form, it moves to a script. The script is, of course, the basis of the entire episode at this point. Sometimes it can go through several drafts as the story is perfected. You can read the first draft of "Goo Goo Grief" below. If you haven't read too many scripts before, check out the way different things are noted, like music, sound effects, props, etc. Compare the way the premise, outline and script are written. The script is a whole different piece of work that's designed to be easy for the different artists (storyboard, backgrounds, props, character designers) and voice actors to break down and work with.
