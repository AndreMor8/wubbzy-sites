A Big Week For Wubbzy!

 
This week is a big week for Wubbzy. He is SO EXCITED! This week is the licensing show in New York City. The licensing show is where different companies work together to help Wubbzy create fun stuff like toys and games... and I know you guys are excited about toys.
They'll also be giving out these super-cute postcards that you see pictured. Wish the Wubbers good luck and keep your fingers crossed for lots of good things coming out of the Licensing Show! - Carrie
