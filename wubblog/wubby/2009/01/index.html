How-To Wall Cut Outs


A few weeks ago I posted about some great Wubbzy wall paintings that I came across on Flickr. In response, Ed sent me a link to his blog where he'd done something similar for his daughter Hannah. Aren't they adorable? I love that they opted for Disco-Walden. haha!
Anyway, take a stroll over to Ed's blog (please note that this link will take you AWAY from the Wubblog!). There, he shows you how they created the plywood cutouts step by step, so you can do them at home yourself! Make sure that, if you do, you send them our way.
Thanks for the photos Ed, I hope Hannah is enjoying them! - Carrie

Wubbzy on Twitter


Did you know that Wubbzy has a Twitter account? Check us out! Visit our Twitter page at: www.twitter.com/wubbzyjen . Jen works with Wubbzy very closely in our offices here and answers tons of questions and keeps you up to date on the latest news from Wuzzleberg. Today she's announced a contest, too! See the details below:

A Sketchity Sketch Contest!
Scan and send your sketches to Wubbzy123@gmail.com. Include the illustrator's age, name and a description of the sketch. The winner will be announced on Monday, 1/19/09 and is to be the proud recipient of a Wubbzy DVD.

I can't wait to see all of your sketches! - Carrie
